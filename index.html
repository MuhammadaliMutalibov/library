Atomic Habits

The book Atomic habits (English Atomic habits) was published by James Clear in 2018. The book contains empirical and theoretical teachings about the power of good and bad habits, the causes of success, and the relationship between habits and success. The book is a New York Times bestseller, has sold over 8 million copies worldwide and has been translated into over 50 languages..

The book has 7 main sections, which can be divided into the most basic sections such as "Basic Principles", "4 Rules" and "Additional Techniques". Because, although these sections differ in page size, the content is almost the same size..
